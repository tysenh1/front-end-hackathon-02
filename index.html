The Big Mac


The McDonalds Big Mac originally came out in August of 1968 to feed steel mill workers as the original single patty cheeseburger wasnt filling enough.

The original Special Sauce for the burger started out as 2 different sauces that both performed well. The two sauces were combined in 1972 and renamed to "Big Mac Sauce '72". The recipe was then changed in 1991 before the original recipe was reinstated in 2004.
